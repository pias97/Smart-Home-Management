The given system uses a temperature and ultrasonic sensor. But it can be improved on by adding the
follwoing in case of a fighter submarines:

1) Temperature Sensor: Sensor like this is used to measure and regulate temperature in a submarine for the
occupants.

2) Ultrasonic Sensor: This is used to map the surroundings for enemies or other obstacles.

3) Pressure Module: With this the submarine can warn us if it goes too deep. Which may harm the vehicle.
